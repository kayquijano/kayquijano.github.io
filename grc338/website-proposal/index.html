Introduction

Compact Collective CDs and More

Compact Collective CDs and More is a local music store that sells CDs. We also sell concert tickets and band merch.

Target audience

People of all ages can use this site. People who appreciate and collect CDs will be the target audience.

Music lovers can use this site to find more information on where to visit our store. They also have the option to buy limited CDs and merchandise online. Additionally, there is a list of upcoming concerts in the area that they can buy tickets for in person at the store.
